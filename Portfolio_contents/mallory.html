How to make a Smore sandwhich

  1. Place two graham crackers, one marshmallow and one chocolate peice on a plate.
  2. Take the marshmallow and heat in the microwave or at a campfire.
  3. Place the chocolate square on one graham cracker and the warm marshmallow on the other cracker.
  4. Smash the two graham crackers together and voila, you have a smore sandwhich!
